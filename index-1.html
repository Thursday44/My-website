About Nessa

I am a writer, storyteller and podcaster — a collector of stories who writes both fiction and nonfiction. I believe every person carries a story worth telling, and my work is about creating the space to tell those stories honestly.

Through Bathroom Musings, I explore real stories with a touch of philosophy — the kind of conversations that feel like catching up with a friend who actually makes you think. My writing does the same, just on the page.

If you love wholehearted stories, curious questions, and words that leave you feeling something — you're in the right place.
